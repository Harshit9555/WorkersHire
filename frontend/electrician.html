Electrical Safety Tips

Prevent Electrical Hazards

  • Never overload outlets with too many devices
  • Replace frayed or damaged cords immediately
  • Keep electrical appliances away from water
  • Use surge protectors for expensive electronics
  • Turn off power at the circuit breaker before doing any electrical work
  • Have regular electrical safety inspections

Warning Signs of Electrical Problems

  • Frequent circuit breaker trips
  • Flickering or dimming lights
  • Buzzing sounds from outlets
  • Warm or discolored outlet covers
  • Burning smell from appliances or outlets
  • Mild shock or tingling feeling when touching appliances
