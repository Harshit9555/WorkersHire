For an average-sized room (12x12 feet), our painters typically take 1-2 days to complete the job, including preparation, priming, and applying multiple coats. Larger or more complex rooms may take 2-3 days.

We use premium quality paints from trusted brands like Asian Paints, Berger, Dulux, and Nerolac. We can recommend the best paint type based on your requirements, environment, and budget.

For best results, we recommend moving furniture to the center of the room or to another area. Our team can help with moving larger pieces and will cover all furniture and floors with protective sheets to prevent any paint splatters.
